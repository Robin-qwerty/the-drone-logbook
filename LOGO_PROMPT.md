# App Logo Generation Prompt

## For AI Image Generators (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.)

### Simple Prompt:
```
A minimalist app icon logo featuring a drone battery with a logbook, clean modern design, flat design style, vibrant colors, square format, suitable for mobile app icon
```

### Detailed Prompt (for better results):
```
Create a minimalist app icon logo for "The Drone Logbook" app. The design should feature:
- A stylized LiPo battery (rectangular with cells visible) combined with a small drone silhouette or propellers
- A subtle logbook/document element (lines or pages)
- Clean, modern flat design style
- Vibrant but professional color scheme (suggestions: blue, green, or orange accents)
- Square format (1024x1024px recommended)
- Simple and recognizable at small sizes
- No text, icon only
- Suitable for mobile app store
```

### Alternative Prompts:

**Option 1 (Battery Focus):**
```
Minimalist app icon: stylized drone battery with 4 cells, clean lines, modern flat design, vibrant blue and green colors, square format, mobile app icon style
```

**Option 2 (Drone + Battery):**
```
Simple app logo: small quadcopter drone silhouette above a battery icon, connected by a subtle line or graph, minimalist design, flat colors, square format, app icon style
```

**Option 3 (Logbook Theme):**
```
App icon design: open logbook with battery and drone symbols inside, clean minimalist style, professional colors, square format, suitable for mobile app store
```

## Design Specifications:

- **Size**: 1024x1024px (minimum for Google Play)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (or solid background)
- **Style**: Flat design, minimalist
- **Colors**: Professional but vibrant (avoid too many colors)
- **Complexity**: Simple enough to be recognizable at 48x48px

## Tips:

1. Test the logo at different sizes (especially small) to ensure it's still recognizable
2. Avoid fine details that won't show at small sizes
3. Use high contrast for visibility
4. Consider both light and dark backgrounds
5. Keep it simple - less is more for app icons

## Recommended Tools:

- **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT Plus or Bing Image Creator)
- **Midjourney**
- **Stable Diffusion**
- **Canva** (for manual design)
- **Figma** (for custom design)

## Example Usage:

Copy one of the prompts above and paste it into your preferred AI image generator. You can also combine elements or modify the prompt to better match your vision.
