# Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 (Initial Test Release)

Welcome to The Drone Logbook!

This is the initial test release of the app. Features include:

- **Battery Management**: Track your FPV drone batteries, including capacity, cell count, and purchase dates
- **Cycle Tracking**: Log battery usage cycles and flight times
- **Resistance Monitoring**: Record internal resistance measurements for each cell
- **Maintenance Reports**: Keep track of issues and maintenance notes for your batteries
- **Drone Inventory**: Manage your drone fleet and track usage
- **Data Export**: Export your data for backup and analysis

This is a test release. Please report any issues or bugs you encounter.

Thank you for testing!
